Search Signals

What to lean into, what to screen out

Prioritize newer inventory

Newer rentals and condo sublets are simply more likely to give you the washer/dryer plus open kitchen combination you care about.

Be skeptical of pretty one-bedrooms

A stylish 1BR can still fail if the kitchen is boxed off or the living room cannot comfortably host people.

Search phrases worth noticing

“Island kitchen,” “great room,” “chef’s kitchen,” and “full-size washer/dryer” are usually better signs than generic “renovated.”

Red flags for your use case

“Galley,” “windowed kitchen,” “junior 1,” “alcove studio,” and “laundry in building” should trigger heavier scrutiny or a pass.
